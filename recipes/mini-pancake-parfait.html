Mini Pancake Parfait

Description

Teeny pancakes get the parfait treatment when they’re layered with yogurt, mixed fruit, and pancakes’ bestie: maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon jam or fruit preserves
  • 5 (mini) Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 teaspoon pure maple syrup, or to taste

Directions

  1. For each parfait, layer 1/3 cup vanilla yogurt, 1 tablespoon jam or fruit preserves, 3 mini pancakes, and 1/2 cup mixed berries in a wide-mouthed glass or jar.
  2. Repeat yogurt and berry layers.
  3. Add 2 additional pancakes and drizzle with maple syrup.
